His ability to simplify complex concepts while keeping the atmosphere light and engaging was truly special.


Beyond his impressive teaching skills, Mr. Kabde Sir also brought a certain elegance and charm to the classroom. His presence in the classroom was both inspiring and uplifting, and his departure will undoubtedly leave a noticeable gap.


As we say goodbye, we remember the impact he had on us with great fondness. His approach to teaching science was both innovative and enjoyable, creating an environment where students thrived. We wish him all the best in his new role, knowing that his talent and enthusiasm will continue to make a positive difference wherever he goes.
