Mrs. Sushma Madam

Mrs. Sushma Madam, our beloved Mathematics Teacher, is transferring to another school, and her departure leaves us with heavy hearts. Throughout her time with us, she has shown an exceptional ability to make mathematics not just understandable, but enjoyable. Her teaching style made complex concepts seem simple, turning what many students find challenging into a subject of excitement and curiosity.


Her passion for teaching was evident in every class. Mrs. Sushma brought a unique energy that transformed the classroom into a vibrant learning environment. She made math fun through engaging activities and creative problem-solving exercises. Her motivation inspired us to tackle even the toughest of problems, fostering a sense of confidence and accomplishment among her students.


Beyond her teaching skills, Mrs. Sushma’s personality made a lasting impact. She knew how to balance seriousness with fun, ensuring that learning never felt tedious. Her sense of humor and approachable demeanor made it easy for students to ask questions and seek help, creating a supportive atmosphere where everyone felt valued.


As she embarks on this new journey, we want to express our heartfelt gratitude for all she has done for us. Her dedication to her students and her innovative teaching methods have made her the best math teacher we could ask for. We wish her all the success in her new role and hope she continues to inspire students as she has inspired us.



“Mathematics may not teach us how to add love or minus hate, but it gives us hope that every problem has a solution.”
